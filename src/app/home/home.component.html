How to Convert to or from Roman Numerals

How to Convert from Integer Roman Numerals

Enter any number between 1 and 3999 into the field labeled Number.

The calculator will convert the Integer into a Roman numeral.

How to Convert from Roman Numeral to Integer

You can also enter Roman numerals (letters M, D, C, L, X, V, and I) into the field labeled Roman number.

The calculator will convert it into an Integer.
